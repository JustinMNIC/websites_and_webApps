Random Password Generator

Documentation

This is a simple random password generator. It generates a random password based on the settings you choose. You can choose the length of the password, whether to include uppercase, lowercase, numbers and special characters. You can also copy the generated password to your clipboard.


How to use

Click on the "Generate Password" button to generate a random password. You can also change the settings to generate a password based on your preferences. You can also copy the generated password to your clipboard by clicking on the "Copy" button.


Settings

Password Length - You can choose the length of the password. The minimum length is 5 and the maximum length is 20.


Uppercase - You can choose whether to include uppercase letters in the password.


Lowercase - You can choose whether to include lowercase letters in the password.


Numbers - You can choose whether to include numbers in the password.


Special Characters - You can choose whether to include special characters in the password.


Copy Password

You can copy the generated password to your clipboard by clicking on the "Copy" button.
